The Wealth Club

Privacy Policy

Introduction

The Wealth Club ("we," "our," or "us") operates social media accounts and related automation tools to publish content. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our services.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate our services. This may include publicly available social media content such as video URLs and captions, account identifiers needed to publish content to our social media accounts, and basic usage data related to our automation tools.

How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to publish and manage content across our social media accounts, rewrite and format captions for published content, and host and deliver video content through third-party hosting services.

Third-Party Services

Our services integrate with third-party platforms including TikTok, Instagram, Telegram, Cloudinary, and AI services for caption generation. Each of these services has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Temporary data such as video files and captions are processed and not stored beyond what is needed for publishing.

Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect the information we handle. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through our social media channels.
